GoPanda helps users discover rental vehicles from local rental shops and partners. GoPanda may provide online vehicle discovery, booking assistance, RentalOS tools for rental shops, B2B mobility coordination, and related technology services.

GoPanda acts as a technology and aggregation layer between users and independent rental partners.

## 2. Eligibility

To rent a vehicle through a GoPanda-listed partner, users may be required to:

- be legally eligible to rent and operate the selected vehicle;
- hold a valid driving licence for the required vehicle category;
- provide identity proof if required by the rental partner;
- provide a valid phone number;
- comply with traffic laws and transport rules;
- meet age and document requirements set by the rental partner.

GoPanda and/or the rental partner may refuse service if eligibility, documentation, or verification requirements are not satisfied.

Unless specifically stated otherwise:

- GoPanda is not responsible for vehicle maintenance, insurance, permits, or legal compliance of rental partner vehicles;
- GoPanda is not the direct rental service provider unless explicitly mentioned.

The actual rental transaction is between the user and the respective rental partner/shop.

## 4. Rental Partner Responsibility

Rental partners are responsible for:

- maintaining vehicle condition and roadworthiness;
- ensuring the vehicle is legally available for rental;
- setting prices, deposit requirements, late fees, and document requirements;
- confirming or rejecting booking requests;
- handling vehicle handover and return;
- checking documents;
- collecting and returning security deposits where applicable;
- explaining fuel, damage, delay, challan, and breakdown policies;
- handling cancellation and refund decisions until GoPanda starts collecting payments directly.

## 5. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- providing accurate personal and booking information;
- carrying valid driving licence and identity documents;
- inspecting the vehicle at pickup and reporting visible damage before use;
- using the vehicle lawfully and responsibly;
- returning the vehicle on time and in agreed condition;
- paying rental fees, deposits, late fees, damage charges, challans, fuel charges, or other applicable charges;
- not using the vehicle for illegal, unsafe, racing, overloading, sub-renting, or unauthorized purposes.

Submitting a booking request on GoPanda does not always guarantee confirmed availability. A booking may be considered confirmed only when the rental partner confirms availability and the user completes required verification/payment/deposit steps.

Rental prices, security deposits, late fees, damage charges, fuel rules, and other fees may be set by the respective rental partner.

Users should confirm all charges before pickup.

Cancellation and refund handling is governed by GoPanda's Cancellation and Refund Policy.

## 10. Security Deposit

Rental partners may require a security deposit before vehicle handover. Deposit amount, payment mode, deduction rules, and refund timeline are decided by the rental partner unless otherwise stated.

Deductions may be made for damage, late return, missing accessories, fuel shortage, challans, cleaning, unauthorized use, or other valid rental-related charges.

## 11. Vehicle Handover and Return

At pickup, users should inspect vehicle condition, visible damage, fuel level, odometer, accessories, return time, and emergency contact process.

At return, the rental partner may inspect the vehicle and settle balance, deposit, damage, late fee, or other charges.
