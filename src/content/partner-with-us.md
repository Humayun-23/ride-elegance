

# Partner With GoPanda

GoPanda works with local rental shops and mobility partners to make vehicle rentals more organized, discoverable, and reliable.

If you operate a bike, scooty, car, or travel rental business, GoPanda can help you digitize your supply and connect with customer and business demand.

## Why Partner With GoPanda?

GoPanda is building:

- an online marketplace for customer discovery;
- RentalOS for rental shop operations;
- a B2B mobility network for business vehicle requirements.

## What Rental Partners Can Use

### Online Listing

List your vehicles for customer discovery.

### RentalOS

Use GoPanda's rental shop operating system to manage vehicle catalog, availability, offline/walk-in bookings, customer records, document uploads, handover photos, payments, trip completion, notes, customer flags, and staff access.

### Business Demand

Participate in GoPanda's B2B mobility network for hotels, travel agencies, offices, events, and local businesses.

## Who Can Join?

GoPanda may work with bike rental shops, scooty rental shops, car rental shops, tour/travel operators, local mobility partners, fleet owners, and vehicle rental businesses.

## Partner Responsibilities

Rental partners are responsible for accurate vehicle information, vehicle condition, valid vehicle documents, pricing/deposit rules, customer document verification, handover/return, cancellation/refund handling where payment is collected by partner, lawful rental operations, and responsible use of customer data.

## Current Payment and Refund Model

As of now, GoPanda is operating as an aggregator platform.

If a rental partner collects payment directly from customers, the rental partner is responsible for cancellation, refund, deposit settlement, receipt, and payment dispute handling.

If GoPanda starts collecting payments directly in the future, platform-level rules will be updated.
