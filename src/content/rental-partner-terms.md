

# Rental Partner Terms

These Rental Partner Terms apply to rental shops, vehicle owners, fleet operators, travel operators, and other partners who list vehicles, use RentalOS, or work with GoPanda.

By using GoPanda as a rental partner, you agree to these terms.

## 1. Partner Status

Rental partners are independent businesses or operators.

GoPanda provides technology, discovery, aggregation, and operational tools.

GoPanda does not become the owner, operator, employer, agent, insurer, or legal representative of the rental partner unless separately agreed in writing.

## 2. Partner Responsibilities

Rental partners are responsible for maintaining listed vehicles, ensuring roadworthiness, keeping vehicle documents valid, setting accurate prices/deposit rules, providing accurate listing details, confirming availability honestly, handling customer verification, handover/return, explaining rental terms, and complying with applicable laws.

## 3. Vehicle Listings

Rental partners must ensure listing information is accurate, including vehicle name/model, category, pricing, availability, location, images, deposit requirement, and rental conditions.

GoPanda may remove, hide, or edit listings that appear inaccurate, misleading, unsafe, duplicate, or outdated.

## 4. Bookings

Rental partners must respond to booking requests within a reasonable time and should not confirm a booking unless they reasonably expect to provide the vehicle.

## 5. Payments, Cancellations, and Refunds

As of now, GoPanda is operating as an aggregator platform.

If a rental partner collects payment directly:

- the partner is responsible for issuing receipt/proof;
- the partner is responsible for cancellation decisions;
- the partner is responsible for refund decisions;
- the partner is responsible for security deposit settlement;
- the partner is responsible for payment disputes with the customer.

GoPanda may assist in communication but does not guarantee refund or deposit settlement for payments collected directly by partners.

If GoPanda starts collecting payments directly in the future, updated payment and settlement terms will apply.

## 6. Customer Documents and Data

Partners may receive or access customer information, including phone number, booking details, ID proof, driving licence, handover photo, and payment records.

Partners must use customer data only for legitimate rental purposes, not misuse/sell/share/leak customer data, restrict access to authorized staff, and follow GoPanda's privacy/data handling guidelines.

## 7. RentalOS Usage

Partners using RentalOS must enter accurate information and use the system responsibly. Partners are responsible for actions of their staff using RentalOS.

## 8. Customer Disputes

Partners must handle customer disputes professionally and in good faith. GoPanda may assist in communication or review, but the partner remains responsible for on-ground rental service and direct payments collected by the partner.

## 9. Prohibited Partner Conduct

Partners must not list unavailable vehicles, provide unsafe/illegal vehicles, misuse documents, overcharge beyond agreed terms, refuse valid refunds without reason, provide false information, use GoPanda customer data for unrelated marketing, damage GoPanda's reputation through misconduct, or bypass required platform processes.

## 10. Suspension or Removal

GoPanda may suspend or remove a partner for repeated complaints, unsafe vehicles, document misuse concern, fraud, false listing, payment/refund misconduct, abusive behavior, legal/compliance risk, or violation of these terms.
