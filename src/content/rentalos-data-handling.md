

# RentalOS Data Handling Note

RentalOS is GoPanda's digital operating system for rental shops.

It may help rental partners manage customer records, bookings, document uploads, handover photos, payments, trip completion, notes, flags, and staff access.

Because RentalOS may involve sensitive customer and rental data, proper data handling is important.

## 1. Types of Data Handled

RentalOS may process customer name, phone number, booking details, vehicle details, driving licence records, ID proof records, customer-with-vehicle handover photos, optional handover location metadata, payment records, deposit records, trip completion status, internal customer notes, internal customer flags, and staff activity records.

## 2. Purpose of Data Use

RentalOS data should be used only for legitimate rental shop operations, including customer verification, booking management, vehicle handover, dispute prevention, payment tracking, trip completion, customer history, safety/risk management, and legal/compliance needs.

RentalOS data should not be used for unrelated marketing, personal use, public posting, unauthorized sharing, or illegal purpose.

## 3. Rental Partner Responsibility

Rental partners using RentalOS are responsible for entering accurate data, getting required customer consent where applicable, limiting access to authorized staff, preventing misuse of documents/photos, maintaining confidentiality, reporting misuse/unauthorized access, and following applicable law and GoPanda policies.

## 4. Staff Access

Rental partners should provide RentalOS access only to trusted staff who need it for rental operations.

Staff should not download unnecessary documents, share customer data outside the business, take screenshots for personal use, transfer customer information to unauthorized persons, or use customer data after leaving the shop.

## 5. Document and Photo Handling

Driving licence, ID proof, and handover photos are sensitive records. They should be collected only when required, used only for rental verification/dispute prevention, stored securely, accessed only by authorized users, and deleted/retained based on lawful and reasonable business requirements.

## 6. Customer Requests

Customers may request correction, deletion, or clarification regarding their data. Such requests should be handled according to GoPanda's Privacy Policy, rental partner requirements, legal requirements, and dispute/record needs.

## 7. GoPanda's Role

GoPanda provides RentalOS as a technology tool. Rental partners remain responsible for how their staff use customer data inside their rental business.

GoPanda may restrict access, suspend accounts, or take corrective action if there is suspected misuse, legal risk, or policy violation.
