

# FAQ / Help Center

Find answers to common questions about renting vehicles through GoPanda and GoPanda-listed rental partners.

## 1. What is GoPanda?

GoPanda is a local vehicle rental platform that helps users discover rental vehicles from local rental partners.

GoPanda currently works as an aggregator/technology platform. The actual rental, vehicle handover, deposit, and on-ground service are handled by the respective rental partner/shop.

## 2. Does GoPanda own the vehicles?

No. Vehicles listed on GoPanda are generally owned and operated by independent rental partners/shops.

## 3. What documents are required?

Document requirements may vary by rental partner, but commonly required documents include:

- valid driving licence;
- government identity proof;
- phone number;
- address or local reference if required;
- security deposit if required.

## 4. Is driving licence mandatory?

Yes, for self-drive rentals, a valid driving licence for the relevant vehicle category is generally required.

## 5. Is a security deposit required?

Some rental partners may require a security deposit. The amount, payment mode, deduction rules, and refund timeline are decided by the rental partner.

## 6. Who handles payment?

At the current stage, payments may be handled directly by the rental partner. If GoPanda starts collecting payments directly in the future, payment rules will be updated.

## 7. Who handles cancellation and refund?

As of now, GoPanda is an aggregator platform. Cancellation and refund decisions are handled by the respective rental partner/shop when payment is made directly to them.

GoPanda may assist in communication but does not guarantee refunds for payments collected by rental partners.

## 8. What if the rental partner cancels?

If a rental partner cancels due to unavailability, breakdown, or operational issue, the partner is responsible for handling any refund if they collected payment directly.

GoPanda may try to help find another vehicle, but availability is not guaranteed.

## 9. What if I return the vehicle late?

Late return charges may apply as per rental partner policy. Confirm return time and late fee before pickup.

## 10. Who pays for fuel?

Fuel rules vary by rental partner. Confirm fuel rules before starting the trip.

## 11. What if the vehicle breaks down?

Immediately contact the rental partner and follow their instructions. If unsafe or urgent, contact emergency services, police, or roadside help as required.

## 12. What if there is an accident?

Ensure safety first, contact emergency services if needed, inform the rental partner, inform police where required, and keep photos/documents/communication records.

Damage, insurance, repair cost, and liability handling depend on rental partner terms, vehicle insurance, and applicable law.

## 13. Can I extend my booking?

Extension is subject to vehicle availability and rental partner approval. Additional charges may apply.

## 14. What if vehicle condition is not good?

Inspect the vehicle before accepting it. If there is damage, poor condition, or mismatch, inform the rental partner immediately before starting the trip.

## 15. When will I get my security deposit back?

Security deposit refund timelines are decided by the rental partner after vehicle inspection and settlement of valid charges.

## 16. I am a rental shop owner. How can I partner with GoPanda?

Contact GoPanda to list your vehicles, use RentalOS, or participate in GoPanda's local rental network.

## 17. I am a hotel/travel agency/office/event operator. Can GoPanda help?

Yes. GoPanda is validating a B2B mobility network where businesses can source vehicles through verified rental partners.
