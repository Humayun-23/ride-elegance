

# Cancellation and Refund Policy

This Cancellation and Refund Policy explains how cancellations, refunds, booking changes, no-shows, and partner-side cancellations are handled on GoPanda.

> **Important current status:** GoPanda is currently operating as an aggregator and technology platform. As of now, GoPanda does not collect rental payments directly for most bookings. Payments, advances, deposits, cancellations, and refunds are handled by the respective rental shop/partner according to their own terms. GoPanda may assist in communication, but refund approval and settlement remain with the rental partner when payment is made directly to them.

If GoPanda starts collecting payments directly in the future, this policy will be updated and GoPanda will publish platform-level cancellation and refund rules.

## 1. GoPanda's Current Role

GoPanda helps users discover rental vehicles and connect with rental partners.

At the current stage:

- GoPanda acts as an aggregator/platform;
- vehicles are owned and operated by independent rental partners;
- rental payments may be collected directly by rental partners;
- security deposits may be collected directly by rental partners;
- cancellation and refund decisions are handled by rental partners;
- GoPanda does not guarantee refunds for payments made directly to rental partners.

Users should confirm cancellation and refund rules with the rental partner before making any payment.

## 2. User Cancellation

If a user wants to cancel a booking request or confirmed booking, the user should inform the rental partner and/or GoPanda as early as possible.

Refund eligibility, cancellation charges, and adjustment of advance/token amount will depend on the rental partner's policy.

GoPanda may help communicate the cancellation request but cannot force a rental partner to refund an amount paid directly to them.

## 3. Last-Minute Cancellation by User

For last-minute cancellations, the rental partner may deduct cancellation charges or retain part/full advance amount depending on their policy.

Users should confirm the exact cutoff time with the rental partner before booking.

## 4. No-Show by User

A no-show may happen when a user does not arrive at the pickup location within the agreed time and does not inform the rental partner in advance.

In case of no-show:

- the rental partner may cancel the booking;
- advance/token amount may be non-refundable;
- the vehicle may be released for other customers;
- GoPanda may not be able to restore the booking.

Final no-show rules are decided by the rental partner.

## 5. Cancellation by Rental Partner

A rental partner may cancel a booking due to vehicle breakdown, vehicle unavailability, previous customer delay, document issue, safety concern, pricing/availability error, or operational issue.

If the rental partner cancels after receiving payment directly, refund or replacement handling is the responsibility of the rental partner.

GoPanda may assist in contacting the partner or finding an alternative vehicle, but does not guarantee availability, replacement, compensation, or refund unless GoPanda directly collected payment and agreed to such terms.

## 6. Refunds for Payments Made Directly to Rental Partners

When a user pays a rental partner directly through cash, UPI, bank transfer, wallet, or any other mode:

- refund decisions are made by the rental partner;
- refund amount is decided by the rental partner;
- refund timeline is decided by the rental partner;
- refund method is decided by the rental partner;
- GoPanda is not responsible for refund delays, denial, deduction, or dispute.

Users should collect payment proof, receipt, screenshot, or written confirmation from the rental partner.

## 7. Refunds for Payments Collected by GoPanda in the Future

If GoPanda starts collecting payments directly through the platform, GoPanda will update this policy with clear rules covering cancellation cutoff time, refundable/non-refundable amounts, token advance refund rules, platform fee if any, refund method, refund timeline, failed payment handling, partner cancellation refund process, and no-show rules.

Until such update, users should treat rental partner rules as the primary cancellation and refund terms.

## 8. Security Deposit Refunds

Security deposits are generally handled by the rental partner unless GoPanda clearly states otherwise.

The rental partner may deduct amounts from the security deposit for damage, late return, fuel shortage, missing accessories, challans, penalties, tolls, cleaning, unauthorized use, or other valid rental-related charges.

Security deposit refund timeline and deduction rules should be confirmed directly with the rental partner.

## 9. Booking Modification

Requests to change booking date, time, vehicle, pickup location, or duration are subject to rental partner approval. Additional charges may apply.

## 10. Disputes

If there is a dispute, users may contact GoPanda Support with booking details, rental partner name, payment proof, screenshots/messages, pickup/return details, and issue description.

GoPanda may try to assist, but does not guarantee any specific outcome for payments or deposits handled directly by rental partners.
