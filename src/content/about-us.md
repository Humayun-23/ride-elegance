

# About GoPanda

GoPanda is building a connected platform for local vehicle rentals.

We started with a simple problem: renting a bike, scooty, or car locally is still too dependent on phone calls, scattered WhatsApp messages, gallery photos, uncertain availability, and manual coordination.

GoPanda exists to make this experience more organized for customers, rental shops, and businesses.

## What We Are Building

GoPanda is evolving into a product portfolio with three connected parts:

### 1. Online Marketplace

A discovery platform where customers can find available rental vehicles from local rental partners.

### 2. RentalOS

A digital operating system for rental shops to manage catalog, walk-in bookings, customer records, document uploads, handover proof, payments, trip completion, notes, flags, and staff access.

### 3. B2B Mobility Network

A business vehicle procurement network to help hotels, travel agencies, offices, events, and local businesses source vehicles through verified rental partners.

## Our Core Idea

RentalOS helps rental shops digitize offline vehicle supply.

The marketplace and B2B network route customer and business demand to that verified supply.

Together, GoPanda aims to become a connected supply-demand layer for local vehicle rentals.

## Why GoPanda

Local vehicle rental is fragmented.

Customers often do not know which vehicles are available, what the real price is, which shop to trust, what documents are needed, or how deposits/refunds work.

Rental shops also face manual booking records, scattered documents, unclear payment tracking, no structured customer history, and difficulty managing offline/online demand together.

## Our Mission

To organize local vehicle rentals by helping rental shops digitize operations and helping customers and businesses access reliable rental vehicles more easily.

## Our Current Focus

Our current focus is improving online rental discovery, building RentalOS, validating B2B vehicle requirements, working with local rental partners, and making rental process more transparent.
