

# Trust and Safety

At GoPanda, trust is important because vehicle rentals involve real people, physical vehicles, documents, payments, and on-ground responsibility.

GoPanda is building tools and processes to make local rentals more transparent, organized, and reliable.

## 1. GoPanda's Role

GoPanda is currently an aggregator and technology platform. We help users discover rental vehicles and connect with local rental partners.

The actual vehicle ownership, maintenance, handover, deposit collection, and on-ground rental service are handled by the respective rental partner/shop.

## 2. Verified Rental Partner Approach

GoPanda aims to work with rental partners who can provide real vehicle availability, clear pricing, proper communication, document-based rental process, responsible handover/return, and customer support.

Verification standards may evolve as GoPanda grows.

## 3. Documented Handover

GoPanda encourages rental partners to maintain handover records such as customer verification, driving licence check, vehicle photos, customer-with-vehicle handover photos, pickup/return time, payment/deposit record, and trip completion record.

These records can help reduce disputes between users and rental partners.

## 4. RentalOS for Partner Operations

RentalOS helps rental partners manage vehicle catalog, availability, walk-in bookings, customer records, document uploads, handover photos, payments, trip completion, notes, customer flags, and staff access.

## 5. User Safety Guidelines

Users should carry a valid driving licence, inspect the vehicle before pickup, take photos of existing damage, confirm fuel level and return time, wear helmet/seatbelt where applicable, follow traffic rules, and contact the rental partner immediately in case of breakdown or accident.

## 6. Rental Partner Safety Guidelines

Rental partners should provide roadworthy vehicles, maintain required vehicle documents, explain rental terms clearly, verify documents responsibly, keep records, provide emergency contact information, handle deposits/refunds fairly, and protect user documents/data.

## 7. Breakdown or Accident Support

In case of breakdown or accident, users should immediately contact the rental partner and emergency services where necessary.

GoPanda may assist in communication where possible, but does not guarantee roadside assistance, replacement vehicle, insurance coverage, compensation, or refund unless clearly stated in writing.

## 8. Data and Document Safety

GoPanda understands that driving licence, ID proof, phone numbers, booking history, handover photos, and payment records are sensitive.

We aim to handle such data carefully and encourage rental partners to use customer information only for legitimate rental purposes.

## 9. Reporting a Problem

Users and rental partners can report unsafe vehicle condition, false listing, payment dispute, document misuse concern, accident/breakdown issue, refund/deposit issue, or misconduct.
