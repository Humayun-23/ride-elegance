

# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GoPanda collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects personal information when you use our website, platform, marketplace, RentalOS tools, booking assistance, partner services, or related services.

By using GoPanda, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. About GoPanda

GoPanda is a vehicle rental technology and aggregation platform. We help users discover rental vehicles from local rental partners and provide tools for rental partners to manage operations.

## 2. Information We May Collect

### User Information

- name;
- phone number;
- email address;
- city or location area;
- booking preferences;
- rental dates and times;
- vehicle type preference;
- communication history.

### Identity and Verification Information

Depending on the workflow, GoPanda or the rental partner may collect or process:

- driving licence details;
- identity proof details;
- uploaded ID documents;
- customer-with-vehicle handover photos;
- document verification status;
- consent records.

### Booking and Rental Information

- selected vehicle;
- rental partner/shop;
- pickup and return details;
- booking status;
- advance, balance, deposit, and payment records;
- trip completion status;
- customer notes or internal flags used by rental partners;
- damage or dispute-related records, where applicable.

### Payment Information

As of now, some payments may be handled directly by rental partners. If GoPanda processes payments in the future, we may collect transaction-related information such as payment status, transaction reference, payment amount, payment method, and refund/cancellation status.

We do not intentionally store sensitive card or banking details unless processed through authorized payment providers.

### Location and Device Information

We may collect approximate location/city, optional handover photo location metadata where permission is given, device/browser information, IP address, cookies, analytics data, and usage logs.

## 3. Why We Collect Information

We collect and use information to:

- provide vehicle discovery and booking assistance;
- connect users with rental partners;
- help rental partners manage bookings and operations;
- verify customer eligibility and documentation;
- maintain booking, payment, and handover records;
- prevent fraud, misuse, disputes, and unsafe rentals;
- improve GoPanda services;
- provide customer support;
- send booking-related updates;
- comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

## 4. How Information Is Shared

We may share relevant information with:

- rental partners for booking confirmation, handover, verification, and service delivery;
- trusted service providers for hosting, storage, analytics, messaging, payment processing, support, or security;
- legal/government authorities where required by law or safety needs;
- business successors in case of merger, acquisition, reorganization, or transfer.

## 5. Sensitive Documents and Handover Photos

Driving licence images, ID proofs, handover photos, and customer verification details should be used only for rental verification, dispute prevention, security, compliance, and booking-related purposes.

Rental partners using RentalOS are responsible for ensuring that their staff handle customer documents carefully and only for legitimate rental purposes.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for booking management, dispute resolution, legal compliance, fraud prevention, partner records, audit, and operational purposes.

Users may request deletion or correction, subject to legal, dispute, fraud-prevention, and operational limitations.

## 7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

## 8. User Choices and Rights

Subject to applicable law and operational limitations, users may request access, correction, deletion, withdrawal of consent where applicable, or information about how data is used.

Requests may be sent to GoPanda Support.

## 9. Cookies and Analytics

GoPanda may use cookies or similar technologies to improve performance, understand usage, remember preferences, improve experience, and support security/analytics.

## 10. Children's Privacy

GoPanda's rental services are not intended for minors who are not legally eligible to rent or operate vehicles.

## 11. Third-Party Links

GoPanda may contain links to third-party websites, payment providers, maps, or rental partner pages. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy.
