

# Contact Us

Need help with a booking, rental partner listing, business requirement, or GoPanda partnership?

## General Support

For booking questions, rental support, cancellation issues, refund communication, or general help:

**Email:** [hello@gopanda.in]  
**Website:** [https://gopanda.in]

## Rental Partner Enquiries

If you are a rental shop owner or operator and want to list vehicles or use RentalOS, include:

- shop name;
- city/location;
- vehicle types;
- approximate number of vehicles;
- contact person;
- phone number.

**Email:** [partners@gopanda.in]

## B2B Vehicle Requirements

If you are a hotel, travel agency, office, event operator, or local business with vehicle requirements, include:

- business name;
- city/location;
- vehicle requirement;
- date and duration;
- number of vehicles;
- contact person;
- phone number.

**Email:** [hello@gopanda.in]

## Privacy or Data Requests

For privacy questions, correction requests, deletion requests, or document handling concerns:

**Email:** [hello@gopanda.in]

## Grievance / Escalation

For unresolved support issues, write with your name, phone number, booking details if any, rental partner name if any, issue summary, and screenshots/proof if available.

**Email:** [hello@gopanda.in]

## Important Note

GoPanda is currently an aggregator platform. For payments, deposits, refunds, and vehicle handover handled directly by rental partners, GoPanda may assist in communication but the rental partner remains primarily responsible.
